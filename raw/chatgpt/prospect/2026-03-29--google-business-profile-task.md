1. Go to: https://www.google.com/business/

## ✅ Step 2: Access the Business Profile Settings
There are two ways (Google has updated the interface):

### Option A (Search Method – most common now)
1. In Google, search **your business name**
2. The Business Profile management panel will appear at the top of search
3. Click **“More” (three dots)**  
4. Select **“Business Profile settings”**

---

## ✅ Step 3: Go to Users
1. Click **“People & access”**
2. This shows all current users (owners/managers)

---

## ✅ Step 4: Add You as a Manager
1. Click **“Add”** (or “+ Add user”)
2. Enter **your email address** (use your Gmail)
3. Under **Access level**, choose:
   - **Manager** (recommended for you)
   - *OR* **Owner** (only if you need full control)
4. Click **“Invite”**

---

## ✅ Step 5: You Accept the Invitation
1. Check your email inbox
2. Open the invite from Google
3. Click **“Accept”**

---

## 🔑 Important Notes (Most People Miss These)
- You **must use a Google account (Gmail or Google-linked email)**
- The client must already be a **verified owner**
- If they don’t see “People & access,” they may not have the right permissions
- New managers **cannot remove the primary owner**
