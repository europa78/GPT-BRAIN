**Mike:** When construction a prompt for a LLM , what are best practices to consider ?

**GPT:** When constructing prompts for a Large Language Model (LLM), consider the following best practices to get optimal results:

### 1. **Clarity and Specificity**
- Clearly define the task.
- Avoid ambiguity—be explicit about your expectations.
- Provide context if necessary.

### 2. **Structured Format**
- Use formatting such as bullet points, numbered lists, or clear paragraphs.
- Clearly separate instructions from context or input examples.

### 3. **Task Definition and Examples**
- Clearly state the type of output you want (e.g., summary, translation, classification).
- Include a few representative examples of desired output if possible (few-shot learning).

### 4. **Include Constraints and Guidelines**
- Clearly define length limits, formatting rules, or any content restrictions.
- Mention what to include and explicitly state what should be avoided.

### 5. **Role Assignment**
- Define the role you want the model to adopt (e.g., "you are a financial analyst," "act as a tutor").
- Role instructions improve the relevance and quality of responses.

### 6. **Prompt Length**
- Be concise, but provide sufficient detail.
- Avoid overly long prompts that can confuse the model.

### 7. **Contextual Awareness**
- Provide relevant background information if context is critical.
- If the model should rely on external information, specify clearly.

### 8. **Iterative Refinement**
- Start with a basic prompt and refine based on feedback.
- Experiment and iterate frequently for better results.

### 9. **Tone and Style**
- Explicitly mention if a specific tone or style is required (formal, conversational, professional).

### 10. **Evaluation and Testing**
- Regularly test your prompts with sample queries.
- Evaluate results systematically to ensure they meet your criteria.

### Example of a Well-Constructed Prompt:
```
Role: You are a financial analyst specializing in stocks and commodities.
Task: Provide a concise and professional summary of recent trends in gold and oil markets.
Constraints:
- Limit to 250 words.
- Include data from the past month.
- Mention specific price movements and influential economic events.
- Do not provide investment advice.
```

Applying these best practices consistently ensures your LLM outputs accurate, reliable, and tailored results.
