## ✅ How to Find Similar Companies in ZoomInfo

1. **Upload Your Client List**  
   - Navigate to **Lists → My Lists → Upload List** and import your client CSV.  
   - ZoomInfo matches contacts to companies and builds firmographic/technographic profiles behind the scenes.

2. **Search for Matched Companies**  
   - Go to **Search → Companies**.  
   - Under **"Saved Lists"** or **"Organization List"**, select the list you've just uploaded.

3. **Use the “More Like This” (MLT) Tool**  
   - On the company list results page, click the **“More Like This”** icon next to one of your companies.  
   - ZoomInfo advises other companies with similar **industry, size, location, tech stack**, and **intent signals**.

   This is ZoomInfo’s version of look-alike modeling—it's not explicitly named “Look-Alike Companies” but serves the same purpose.

4. **Define Similar Company Criteria**  
   - You can also use **Company Search filters**:  
     - e.g. Industry = X, Revenue = Y–Z, Employees = A–B, Location = same, Tech = same.  
   - ZoomInfo’s algorithm will pull in companies matching most of these attributes.

5. **Save or Export Recommendations**  
   - After reviewing, save the list (e.g., “Look-Alike List – Q3”) or export it via the **Export** button.
