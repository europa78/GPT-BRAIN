- **Symbol:** A stylized magnifying glass (to represent visibility and discovery) or a location pin (to evoke “local/Google Business” theme).  
- **Color:** Use a bright accent color (e.g., vibrant green or electric blue) that stands out against neutral backgrounds.

**3. Text**  
- **Company Name (or Initials):**  
  - Choose a bold, modern sans-serif font (like Montserrat, Poppins, or Raleway).  
  - If your company name is short, include it in full. If it’s long, consider a shortened version or initials.  
  - Example: “YourBiz Setup” or “GB Profile Setup”  
- **Tagline (Optional):** A short phrase like “Local Presence Made Easy.” Keep the font smaller and lighter.

**4. Colors**  
- **Primary Color:** A bold hue that reflects energy and trust (e.g., bright blue #007BFF or teal #008080).  
- **Secondary Color:** Gray (#555555) or black for text to maintain readability.  
- **Accent/Highlight:** White background or white text to contrast with the bold color.

**5. Overall Feel**  
- Clean, professional, and approachable.  
- Showcases that you specialize in Google-related services (the icon suggests location/search).  
- The text is easy to read even at small sizes (like on social media profiles).
