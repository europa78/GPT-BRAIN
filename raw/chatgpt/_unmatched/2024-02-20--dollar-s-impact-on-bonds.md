### 2. **LLC Elected as S Corporation**
- If an LLC elects to be taxed as an S corporation, the interest income it earns would still flow through to the owners and be taxed at their personal income tax rates. ### 3. **Interest Income Taxation**
- Interest income, whether earned through an LLC or personally, is generally taxed as ordinary income at the federal level. This means it's subject to the same tax rates as other forms of income, not at a special or separate rate.
