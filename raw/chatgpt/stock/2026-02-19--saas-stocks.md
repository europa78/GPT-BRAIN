**Enterprise apps / workflow:** CRM, NOW, TEAM, WDAY, ASAN, MNDY

**Data/AI platforms:** SNOW, PLTR, AI, MDB

**Commerce / payments:** SHOP, TOST, BILL, PAYC

- Options note: often clean for trend trades; watch macro prints.

## 2) A simple “options playbook” by regime
**Risk-on / falling rates:** lean long via **call spreads** (45–75 DTE), sell put spreads on pullbacks (defined risk).  
**Chop / range:** **iron condors** on Tier-1 names (tight markets), or **calendars** around known catalysts.  
**Risk-off / volatility spike:** avoid naked short gamma; use **put debit spreads** or **collars** if long stock.

## 3) What actually separates winners inside SaaS (what to listen for)
- **Seat-based vs consumption-based** revenue sensitivity (consumption tends to whip harder).  
- **NRR / GRR trend + new logo adds** (durability vs saturation).  
- **Margins + FCF** (multiple support when growth slows).  
- **Guidance quality** (SaaS trades guidance more than quarters).
