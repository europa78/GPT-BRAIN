**Mike:** how can I improve this method of searching through Facebook for business owners who need marketing ?

**GPT:** You’re on the right track by manually searching posts and pages with keywords like *“marketing help,” “advertising my business,” “need SEO”*. But Facebook’s search is limited and noisy, so here are several ways you can improve the method to consistently find high-quality business owners who need marketing:

---

## 1. Optimize Search Queries
- **Use multiple intent-based phrases**, not just keywords:
  - `"looking for marketing help"`
  - `"need help with advertising"`
  - `"ads not working"`
  - `"boost my business"`
  - `"SEO help"`
  - `"promote my business"`
- **Pair with “my business”** → catches posts where owners talk about their own company.
- **Add local modifiers** (Fort Lauderdale, Miami, Florida) when you want to prospect regionally.
- Rotate these queries in batches to avoid repeating the same pool of results.

---

## 2. Leverage Facebook Filters
- **Filter by “Posts”** (not People/Groups) to surface requests for help.
- **Use “Most Recent”** instead of “Top” to capture fresh posts from active owners.
- **Check Groups** where SMB owners hang out:
  - “Small Business Owners [City]”
  - “Entrepreneur Startups”
  - “Women in Business”
  - “Local Business Networking [Region]”

These groups often contain posts like *“Does anyone know a good SEO person?”*.

---

## 3. Profile & Page Validation
When you find a post:
- Click through to confirm they are an **owner/manager** (bio often says “Owner at X”).
- Look for **business page links** attached to their profile.
- Add them to your **Business Leads table** only if they are a legitimate SMB (exclude other agencies and spam accounts).

---

## 4. Systematic Capture of Engagement Data
- Always click **reaction counts** and **“All comments”** (not just “Most relevant”).
- Record:
  - People who **liked/loved** (interested audience).
  - People who **commented/tagged** (network referrals).
  - Marketing agencies commenting (good for competitive intelligence, but not leads).

---

## 5. Automate Data Extraction
Right now you’re doing this manually. To scale:
- **Agent Mode / Automation scripts** can:
  - Run your saved search queries daily.
  - Auto-export profile names, links, and engagement into a Google Sheet.
- Tools like **PhantomBuster** or **Bardeen** can scrape results after you’ve filtered posts in your logged-in account.
- Pair with enrichment (ZoomInfo, Clay) to append phone/email when available.

---

## 6. Refine Targeting with Location + Industry
- Layer queries like:
  - `"need marketing help" Miami`
  - `"SEO help" Fort Lauderdale`
  - `"advertising not working" salon`
- This narrows down results to your real buyer personas instead of global noise.

---

✅ **Bottom line:**  
Instead of only keyword hunting, expand to intent-based phrases, use filters for freshness, validate profiles as SMB owners, and build automation into your workflow. Combined with local + industry keywords, you’ll consistently capture 20–30 high-quality leads per run with much less manual work.
