## What “NFPA 96 Certification” Really Means (and Caveats)

Before jumping to numbers, a few caveats:

- **NFPA 96 itself does *not* issue a national “license or certification”**. Rather, NFPA 96 is a standard (Standard for Ventilation Control and Fire Protection of Commercial Cooking Operations). Local jurisdictions, fire marshals, AHJs (Authority Having Jurisdiction) adopt and enforce it.

- Different certifying organizations exist (e.g. IKECA, CHDCA, Phil Ackland, Hood-Tech Institute, PWNA). Some are better recognized by local fire authorities than others.
