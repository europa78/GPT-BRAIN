### **2. Table: QTY, Product, Unit Price, Total**
**Issues:**
- Misalignment in the "QTY," "PRODUCT," "UNIT PRICE," and "TOTAL" columns.
- Confusion in the pricing—"$250.00" listed but discounted to "$200.00," with no breakdown in the discount section.

**Suggestions:**
- Use clear headings and align content properly within the columns.

|-----|------------------------------------------|------------|----------|--------|
| 1   | GBP (Google Business Profile) Creation  | $250.00    | 20%      | $200.00|

```
Subtotal:      $250.00  
Discount:      -$50.00  
Total:         $200.00
```

One-time setup fee for creating and optimizing a Google Business Profile (GBP).
