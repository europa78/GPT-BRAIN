### Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Template in DocuSign:
1. **Log in to DocuSign**: 
   - Go to [DocuSign](https://www.docusign.com/) and log in to your account.

2. **Access the Templates Section**:
   - From the DocuSign dashboard, click on **"Templates"** in the main navigation bar.

3. **Create a New Template**:
   - Click the **"New"** button, then select **"Create Template"** from the dropdown menu.

4. **Add Template Information**:
   - **Name Your Template**: Give your template a clear, descriptive name.
   - **Description**: Add a description for internal reference, so others understand its purpose.

5. **Upload a Document**:
   - Click **"Upload"** to add the base document for your template. This could be a PDF, Word document, or other supported file formats.

6. **Add Roles**:
   - In the "Add Recipients" section, define roles (e.g., "Signer," "Approver," "Manager"). Roles allow you to assign placeholders for recipients when using the template.

7. **Add Fields**:
   - Drag and drop fields (e.g., Signature, Initials, Date) onto the document where the recipients need to take action.
   - Adjust the fields for specific roles by selecting the role from the dropdown menu in the field editing toolbar.

8. **Set Permissions and Advanced Options** (Optional):
   - You can assign custom permissions for roles, set signing orders, or pre-fill certain fields.

9. **Save the Template**:
   - Once you’ve completed your setup, click **"Save and Close"**. Your template is now ready for use.

### Using Your Template:
- To use the template, go to **Templates**, select your desired template, and click **"Use"**. Fill in the recipient information and send the document for signing.
