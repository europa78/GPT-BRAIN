## **Step 1: Key Criteria for Ideal Product Opportunities**

### Essential Criteria:
- **Price Arbitrage:**  
  Find products available inexpensively in certain regions or states (e.g., Georgia, Mississippi) that sell for significantly higher prices in premium or distant markets (e.g., California, New York).
  
- **Lightweight and Compact:**  
  Reduces shipping and storage costs; small or flat-pack items are ideal.

- **High Profit Margins:**  
  Aim for items with at least a 30% profit margin, accounting for fulfillment fees, shipping, and platform charges.

- **Non-perishable and Durable:**  
  Products should withstand shipping and storage conditions.

- **Market Demand Stability:**  
  Focus on consistent consumer demand rather than fleeting trends.
