### Understanding Japanese Candlesticks

Each candlestick represents a specific time period (e.g., one day, one hour) and shows four key pieces of information:
- **Open Price**: The price at the beginning of the period.
- **Close Price**: The price at the end of the period.
- **High Price**: The highest price during the period.
- **Low Price**: The lowest price during the period.

The **body** of the candlestick is the area between the open and close prices, while the **wicks** (or shadows) represent the high and low prices.

### Manifestations of Supply Imbalance
