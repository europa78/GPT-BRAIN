## 1. Start with **Lead Search** (people) using “family office” + VC angle

In Sales Navigator:

1. Click **Lead Filter** (Advanced search → Leads).
2. Set your **core filters** first:
   - **Geography**: e.g. United States, Canada, Europe, LATAM, etc.
   - **Seniority level**:  
     - `Owner`, `Partner`, `CXO`, `Director`, `VP`.
   - **Company headcount**:  
     - Start with `1–10` and `11–50` (most family offices live there).
   - **Company type**:  
     - Check `Privately held`, `Partnership`, maybe `Self-employed`.  
     - Uncheck `Public company`, `Nonprofit`, `Government`.

3. Now use the **Keywords** field (top of the filter panel) with Boolean:

   ```text
   "family office" AND ("venture capital" OR "VC" OR "early stage" OR "growth equity" OR "startup investing")
   ```

   This looks for profiles that mention *both* “family office” *and* some form of venture/VC language. citeturn0search2turn0search8  

4. Tighten with **Title** filter (job titles of decision-makers):

   Add titles like (you can add multiple):
   - `Chief Investment Officer`
   - `CIO`
   - `Managing Director`
   - `Managing Partner`
   - `Head of Investments`
   - `Investment Director`
   - `Principal`
   - `Portfolio Manager`

5. Optional but powerful: **Posted content keywords** = `venture capital`, `VC`, `startups`, `early-stage`, etc.  
   - This surfaces people at family offices who actively *talk* about VC.

6. Hit **Search**, then:
   - **Save** good fits to a list: e.g. `Family Offices – VC Focus (Leads)`.
