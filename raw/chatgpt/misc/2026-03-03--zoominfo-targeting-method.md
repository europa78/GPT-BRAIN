## 3) Make it *smart*: layer Intent + Scoops (this is where ZoomInfo becomes unfair)
### Step C — Intent (prioritize who’s actively shopping)
Use **Intent Topics** related to retirement plans. Depending on what ZoomInfo exposes in your plan, look for topics like:
- 401(k), retirement plan, plan administrator
- employee benefits, benefits administration
- ERISA, fiduciary, recordkeeper, TPA
- payroll / HRIS (sometimes a strong adjacent signal)

Then:
- Filter contacts/companies by **Intent surging** in the last **7–30 days**
- Sort by **Intent strength**

### Step D — Scoops (timing triggers)
Add Scoops filters like:
- New CFO / HR leader hired
- Expansion / new location
- M&A
- Compliance / operational change signals

These are great “change” moments that match *“willing to talk / looking for a change.”*

## 4) Turn it into a repeatable ZoomInfo workflow (fast execution)
If you have **ZoomInfo Workflows**:
- **Trigger:** Company enters list + Intent increases OR new Scoop appears  
- **Action:** Enrich → push to CRM/Salesloft → assign owner → enroll in cadence

If you don’t have Workflows, you can still do it manually:
- Refresh the saved search weekly
- Pull only “newly found” contacts
- Sequence by persona list
