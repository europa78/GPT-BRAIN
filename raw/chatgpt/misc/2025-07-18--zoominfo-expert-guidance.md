### 🔍 **Definition: Streaming Intent (ZoomInfo)**

ZoomInfo’s **Streaming Intent** shows you **which companies are actively researching topics relevant to your services right now**—like “SEO,” “digital marketing,” or “CRM software.” It’s powered by ZoomInfo’s partnerships with third-party publishers and their own network of B2B content sites.

Key signals include:
- **Article reads**
- **Whitepaper downloads**
- **Search queries**
- **Comparison page visits**
- **Video views (webinars, product demos, etc.)**

This intent is **topic-based**, and ZoomInfo uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to assign scores to companies showing higher-than-usual interest in a topic.

---
