### **What Are Paid Search Ads?**  
When you enter a search query on Google, the Search Engine Results Page (SERP) displays both organic and paid results.  

Paid search results appear at the top and are marked with an "Ad" label. To run these ads and direct traffic to your site, sign up for a Google Ads account. You only pay when someone clicks on your ad and visits your website.  

---

### **How Google Ads Helps Your Business**  
Google Ads provides access to the **Google Keyword Planner**, a tool that helps you choose the best keywords for your campaign. When selecting keywords, consider:  
- **Relevance** – Do these keywords match what your audience is searching for?  
- **Search Volume** – How frequently are these terms searched?  
- **Cost** – Lower-volume keywords tend to be more affordable in ad auctions.  

Google uses a **Quality Score** (on a scale of 1–10) to determine your ad’s effectiveness. This score is based on:  
1. The quality of your selected keywords  
2. How relevant your ad is to the keywords  
3. The user experience of your landing page  

A higher Quality Score helps lower costs and improves ad performance.
