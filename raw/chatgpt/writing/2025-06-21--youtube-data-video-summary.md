- **Full-stack Personalization:** Combining Sendspark’s AI name-swap with firmographic context from Clay creates “hand-crafted” vibes—while still firing hundreds of emails per seat per day.

### Pro Tips & Cautions

1. **Warm your domains** in Smartlead for at least two weeks before volume sends to protect deliverability.

4. Push positive replies straight into your CRM using Clay’s native Pipedrive/HubSpot actions, or a Make/Zapier bridge.
