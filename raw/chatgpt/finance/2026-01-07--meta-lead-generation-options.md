**GPT:** Meta doesn’t have a true 1:1 equivalent to **Google Local Services Ads (LSA)** (the “Google Guaranteed” pay-per-lead product that sits at the very top of search results).

But Meta *does* have a few “LSA-like” options depending on what you mean by “similar”:

## The closest Meta equivalents

### 1) **Facebook / Instagram Lead Ads (Instant Forms)**
This is the most “LSA-like” in the sense that:
- the lead is captured **inside Meta** (fast conversion, low friction)
- you can optimize for **leads** and get a steady flow of form fills
- you can send leads straight to a CRM (or email/SMS) and follow up quickly

**Best for:** services where speed + volume matters (home services, med spas, legal, business services, etc.).

### 2) **“Click to Message” Ads (Messenger / Instagram DM / WhatsApp)**
For many SMBs, this outperforms forms because it feels like texting.
- prospect taps → conversation starts immediately
- you can automate first replies with FAQs, qualification questions, booking links, etc.

**Best for:** appointment-based businesses and anything where trust/objections matter.

### 3) **Meta Business Suite “Appointments” / “Bookings” (where available)**
Some businesses can use Meta’s native booking flows (mostly via Pages/IG). It’s not an ad product like LSA, but combined with lead/message ads it can feel similar because you’re driving prospects into a native booking action.

## What Meta does *not* really have (vs LSA)
- No universal “verified/guaranteed” badge equivalent to **Google Guaranteed**
- No broad “pay per lead with dispute system” product that mirrors LSA’s mechanics across service categories in the same way

1) What industry are you selling leads for? (roofing, remodeling, medical, legal, etc.)

If you want a quick default recommendation for SMB lead gen on Meta: **Click-to-Message ads** + an automated qualification flow + a booking link is usually the closest feel to “LSA speed” and conversion intent.
