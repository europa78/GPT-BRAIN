### Quick prospecting tips
1. **Segment your list first** – tailor your pitch to *exactly* what they build (e.g., “We help fiberglass pool installers rank #1 for ‘fiberglass pools near me’ in less than 60 days”).  
2. **Use project value to set your service tier** – luxury custom firms can afford aggressive PPC retainers; vinyl-liner builders may start with GBP optimization only.  
3. **Show concrete ROI examples** – pool builders love visuals; lead with call-tracking screenshots, drone footage ads, or before-after ranking graphs.  
4. **Work the shoulder seasons** – many builders scramble for leads post-summer; time outreach when their pipeline dips.

Leverage these categories to create hyper-relevant call scripts and email sequences, boosting your connection rate and positioning Simply The Best Digital as the specialist agency for pool contractors.
