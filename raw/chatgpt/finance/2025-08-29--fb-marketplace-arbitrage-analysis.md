4. **Offer Delivery** – For heavy items (sofas, washers, bikes) include delivery within a 15‑mile radius for a small fee. Delivery expands buyer pool and speeds up sales.
5. **Timing & Seasonality** – Post yard tools and bikes in early spring/fall when demand peaks; list electronics and laptops before school semesters or holidays to maximize price.
