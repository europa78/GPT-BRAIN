## 3 | Demand-Gen Ops & Measurement

1. **Attribution/Tracking Stack**  
   - Google Tag Manager → GA4 events → CRM (HubSpot/SFDC) closed-loop reporting  
   - UTM conventions documented and enforced via naming macros  
   - Weekly pipeline-by-source report aligns Sales & Marketing.

2. **Lead Scoring & Routing**  
   - Score on firmographic fit (size, industry, tech stack) + behavioral intent (visited pricing, watched ≥ 50 % of demo)  
   - Automatically assign ≥ MQL threshold to SDR queue within 5 minutes; others go to nurture.

3. **Lifecycle Nurtures**  
   - 5-touch email sequence, 1 x retargeting branch, personalized by persona (CISO vs. Data Engineer).  
   - Add “hot intent” triggers: if contact downloads security whitepaper + visits pricing, fast-track to SDR.

4. **Quarterly Growth Experiments**  
   - Chatbot A/B: static “Book demo” vs. conversational qualifier.  
   - Landing-page personalization with reverse-IP firmographics.  
   - CRO sprints on high-intent pages (headline, social proof modules, exit pops).

5. **North-Star Metrics**  
   - Pipeline sourced (Marketing)  
   - Qualified lead-to-SQL conversion %  
   - Sales cycle length  
   - Blended CAC vs. target (≤ 40 % ACV)  
   - Net revenue retention (post-sale upsell health)
