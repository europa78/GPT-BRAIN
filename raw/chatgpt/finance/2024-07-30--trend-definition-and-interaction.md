### Types of Trends
Three primary trends are widely recognized in technical analysis: 
1. **Primary Trends**: These are long-term trends that last for more than a year. They represent the major market movements and are crucial for long-term investors.
2. **Secondary Trends**: These are shorter-term trends lasting from a few weeks to a few months. They often move against the primary trend and are seen as corrections or consolidations.
3. **Minor Trends**: These are short-term movements lasting from a few days to a few weeks. They are often volatile and influenced by daily market news and events.
