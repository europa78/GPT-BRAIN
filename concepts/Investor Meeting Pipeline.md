# Investor Meeting Pipeline
> One-liner: the one-person IR workflow — build a right-sized investor target list, run compliant multi-channel outreach, convert interest into confirmed virtual meetings with the CEO; deliverable is the CEO's calendar, not a deck.

## The workflow (settled form, 2025-07 → 2026-02)
1. **Target list (~150 names):** [[ZoomInfo]] + LinkedIn investor search (2026-02-21); segment by check size — the [[ALBT]] lesson: *lists full of investors too large for the company are dead lists; re-target smaller RIAs.*
2. **Collateral:** elevator pitch, HTML email series, interactive landers ([[mPWR]] assets).
3. **Outreach:** [[SalesLoft]] cadences (+[[GoHighLevel]] automation option), Reg-compliant framing.
4. **Convert:** interest → scheduled virtual presentation with the CEO.

## Plan-writing lessons (CEO-facing)
- The CEO wants a *simple* plan: Mike rejected three drafts as too complex — final form has no platform names, no daily quotas, no named funds; structure + safeguards only, numbers left open (2025-07-16).
- Success story used as social proof in prospecting: SRM ticker $0.30 → $10 during engagement ([[Clay]] look-alike hunt).
