# Cold Outreach Playbook
> One-liner: what actually recurred across 3+ years of outreach — ZoomInfo-targeted email templates, SalesLoft cadences, competitor-fear subject lines, HTML email formats, and per-prospect customization at scale.

## The recurring engine
1. **List building:** [[ZoomInfo]] ICP lists (see "ICP client list for ZOOMinfo"), [[Clay]] enrichment, scrape-first sources (Sunbiz new LLCs, Instagram, Craigslist-era) — see [[Lead Enrichment Pipeline]].
2. **Message: pain-first, competitor-fear hook.** The proven subject/opening pattern: *"Local Homebuyers Are Searching Google… But They're Finding Your Competitors"* (Rucco, 2026-03).
3. **Delivery:** [[SalesLoft]] multi-channel cadences (email + call + LinkedIn); HTML email series for investor outreach ([[mPWR]] alt emails 1–3).
4. **Customization at scale:** "customized outreach emails" and "ladder method" threads (2023) — template + per-prospect variable blocks.
5. **Escalation:** email → proposal ([[Proposal Factory]]) in the same thread when interest shows.

## Channel notes
- Email is the spine; LinkedIn (Sales Navigator, investor search) and cold calls wrap around it.
- Same playbook serves both [[STB Digital]] client-getting and [[mPWR]] investor-getting — one engine, two ICPs.
